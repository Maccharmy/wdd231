Akwa Ibom Chamber of Commerce

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Akwa Ibom Chamber of Commerce

This name represents a professional organization dedicated to supporting businesses and fostering economic growth in Akwa Ibom State. It emphasizes locality and purpose, making it recognizable and relevant.

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a central hub for business owners, investors, and residents. It will provide resources, event information, membership details, and a directory of local enterprises to encourage networking and community engagement.

Target Market

Business owners, corporate executives, entrepreneurs, and community members in Akwa Ibom and surrounding regions.

Site Goals

  • Enhance member engagement through valuable resources and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses and encourage sustainable economic development.
  • Strengthen the chamber’s reputation as a trusted authority in local commerce.

User Personas

  • Small Business Owner: A boutique owner seeking marketing support and networking opportunities.
  • Corporate Executive: A company leader interested in partnerships and growth prospects.
  • New Resident: A newcomer looking for directories, events, and community services.

Scenarios

  • How can I apply for membership and what benefits are included?
  • Where can I find upcoming workshops and networking events?
  • Which businesses are currently listed in the chamber’s directory?

SEO Plan

  • Use relevant keywords such as “Akwa Ibom business,” “commerce,” and “networking.”
  • Verify the site on Google Business Profile.
  • Secure backlinks from member businesses.
  • Embed Google Analytics on all pages.

Design Brief

Primary Color: Emerald Green (#006400) – headings and navigation.

Secondary Color: Golden Yellow (#FFD700) – accents and highlights.

Background: Light Gray (#f4f4f9) – page background.

Text: Charcoal (#333333) – body text.

Font Family: Roboto, sans-serif.
